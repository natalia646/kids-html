About Rainbow Parties

The company was founded by three moms of little boys and girls aimed to share their experience of throwing parties and make parents lives easier. Alice, Robin, and Lucy, three founders of the company, had quite a journey planning parties for their children and faced all the possible complications and challenges. It was not an easy task, so in the end, they had to figure out the easiest way for parents to relax while kids are having fun and developed their own methods of planning, organizing, and carrying out an original party for children. That's how the idea for the Rainbow Parties was born. By figuring out innovative ways of organizing and planning kids' parties, our team helps parents save money without sacrificing their kids' holiday experiences. This way, children's holidays can remain relaxing for the entire family. While our event-managers and chefs handle the fun part, parents are chilling, talking to other parents, and just have a good time.

Back to Classics

Classics are proven with time and generations and never get old, no matter how much smartphones and social media conquer kids' minds. Great food, amazing music, designed theme, and active games have been the main ingredients of a fun party for many centuries and generations, and surprisingly, things haven't changed much. Of course, classics have to be neither boring nor old-fashioned. At Rainbow Parties, we combine the eternal basics with new trends and technologies, adapting time-proven methods to the likes of the latest generation. Moreover, we will talk to your kid to find out what's his or her interest are, so we can build up the main theme around topics your child cares about.
